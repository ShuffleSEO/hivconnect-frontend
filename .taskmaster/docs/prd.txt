# HIV Connect Central NJ - Product Requirements Document

## Project Overview
HIV Connect Central NJ is a comprehensive web-based resource platform designed to connect individuals with HIV care, testing, treatment, and support services across Middlesex, Somerset, and Hunterdon counties in New Jersey. The website serves as a centralized hub for the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program network, providing accessible, inclusive, and culturally competent information and service coordination.

## Core Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Centralized Resource Access**: Create a single point of access for all HIV-related services across the three-county region
2. **Service Coordination**: Improve coordination between Ryan White Parts A-D providers and community partners
3. **Health Equity**: Eliminate barriers to care through accessible design, multilingual support, and inclusive messaging
4. **Emergency Support**: Provide immediate access to crisis resources and emergency services
5. **Community Engagement**: Foster connections between people living with HIV and available support networks

### Target Audiences
- **Primary**: People living with HIV/AIDS across all demographics
- **Secondary**: At-risk individuals seeking prevention services (testing, PrEP)
- **Tertiary**: Family members, caregivers, and support networks
- **Quaternary**: Healthcare providers and case managers seeking referral resources

## Technical Architecture

### Framework Requirements
- **Core Framework**: Astro 5.x with TypeScript for optimal performance and SEO
- **Interactive Components**: React islands for search, mapping, and dynamic features
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with accessibility-first design system
- **Database**: Supabase for provider directory and content management
- **Search**: Advanced filtering with Fuse.js or Algolia integration
- **Maps**: Google Maps API for location services and provider mapping
- **Performance**: Static site generation with CDN delivery targeting <2.5s LCP

### Data Structure
- **Provider Schema**: Comprehensive provider data including contact, services, eligibility, hours
- **Service Taxonomy**: Standardized categorization across medical care, support services, and prevention
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates, accessibility features, transportation options
- **Multilingual Content**: Translation framework supporting English and Spanish initially

## Core Features & Functionality

### 1. Provider Directory & Search
- **Advanced Search**: Text search across provider names, services, and descriptions
- **Multi-Filter System**: County, service type, language, insurance, urgency level
- **Interactive Map**: Real-time provider locations with service-based color coding
- **Provider Profiles**: Detailed pages with contact, services, hours, accessibility info
- **Real-time Availability**: Integration with provider systems for appointment availability

### 2. Service Categories

#### HIV Testing & Prevention
- **Testing Locations**: Comprehensive directory of testing sites with rapid testing options
- **At-Home Testing**: Information and ordering for home test kits
- **PrEP Services**: Pre-exposure prophylaxis counseling and medication access
- **STD Testing**: Co-located sexually transmitted disease testing and treatment

#### HIV Treatment & Medical Care
- **Treatment Centers**: HIV specialty care and infectious disease providers
- **Same-Day Initiation**: Providers offering immediate treatment start
- **Telehealth Options**: Remote care services and virtual consultations
- **Comprehensive Care**: Dental, vision, mental health, and specialty services

#### Support Services
- **Case Management**: Personal navigation and care coordination
- **Mental Health**: Individual and group counseling, trauma-informed care
- **Housing Assistance**: Emergency housing, rental assistance, transitional programs
- **Financial Support**: Emergency financial aid, benefits enrollment, insurance help
- **Transportation**: Medical appointment transportation and travel assistance
- **Legal Services**: Discrimination, benefits appeals, advance directives

### 3. Emergency & Crisis Support
- **Crisis Hotlines**: One-click access to 988, HIV/AIDS hotline, local crisis services
- **Quick Exit**: ESC key functionality for safety in unsafe browsing situations
- **Emergency Services**: After-hours care, urgent medical needs, crisis intervention
- **Safety Resources**: Domestic violence, substance abuse, mental health crisis support

### 4. Resource Library
- **Educational Materials**: HIV basics, treatment information, prevention resources
- **Downloadable Resources**: Forms, benefit applications, educational handouts
- **Video Content**: Testimonials, educational videos, provider introductions
- **Multilingual Resources**: Spanish translations of key materials

### 5. Community Features
- **Support Groups**: Directory of peer support groups and meeting information
- **Events Calendar**: Community events, health fairs, educational workshops
- **Peer Navigation**: Connection to peer navigators and community health workers
- **Success Stories**: Anonymous testimonials and inspiring community stories

## Design & User Experience Requirements

### Accessibility Standards
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: Full accessibility across all features and content
- **Screen Reader Support**: Comprehensive ARIA labels and semantic markup
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Complete site functionality via keyboard only
- **Visual Accessibility**: High contrast mode, font scaling, color blind support
- **Cognitive Accessibility**: Simple language, clear navigation, progress indicators

### Inclusive Design Principles
- **Person-First Language**: Respectful terminology throughout all content
- **Cultural Competency**: Imagery and messaging inclusive of all communities
- **Reading Level**: 8th grade maximum reading level for all public-facing content
- **Language Support**: English and Spanish with framework for additional languages
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design optimized for all device types

### Brand & Voice
- **Empowering**: Focus on strength, resilience, and personal agency
- **Welcoming**: Inclusive messaging that affirms all identities
- **Trustworthy**: Professional credibility balanced with warmth
- **Hopeful**: Emphasize health, wellness, and thriving communities
- **Accessible**: Clear, jargon-free communication
